Chroma Keying

Chroma Keying | color key
Automatic image segmentation in order to separate the object in the foreground from the background. With the background color should be continuous and uniform - as a rule, is either green or blue, depending on what color does not exist on the site. The Separated image then is superimposed on another background - for example, on the photo, video, or synthesized image. The method of the color key is widely distributed in the cinema and television.

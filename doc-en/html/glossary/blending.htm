Blending

Blending | Mixing
Mixing two colors in the RGB model for the third. Used to overlay one image to another using the different effects of transparency.

There are many different types of mixing. Enter the following notations:
R1, G1, B1, A1 - respectively represent the red, green, blue and alpha components of the first color.
R2, G2, B2, A1 is similar to the second color.
Examples of mixing:
Alpha Blending - the alpha blending: (R1*A1+R2*(1-A1), the G1*A1+G2*(1-A1, B1*A1+B2*(1-A1))
Additive Blending - summation: (R1+R2, G1+G2, B1+B2)
Modulate Blending - multiplication: (R1*R2, G1*G2, B1*B2)

See also Alpha, Color, Color Model.

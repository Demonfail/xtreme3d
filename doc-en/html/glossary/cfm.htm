The CFM

Constraint Force Mixing | Power, limiting the mixing
Parameter, which determines the depth of the interpenetration between the bodies. If the CFM is set to zero, the connection will be tough. If the CFM is a positive number, then there is an opportunity to break the connection "offset". In other words the connection becomes "soft" and "softness" will grow with the increase in the CFM.
It should be noted that only with the help of the CFM may somehow simulate the soft body in the physics of solids.
This term is used only in the physical ODE engine.

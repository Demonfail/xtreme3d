The method of achieving the terrain visibility on a flat surface. For the implementation of the bump mapping use different types of textures:

- Maps of heights, where the ������� dictates the height in the appropriate location on the map. The textures look like black and white. The lighter the Texel, the higher is the relevant part of the surface.

Normal linear transformation are compared with the colors. Each coordinate (x, y, z) for the corresponding color (r, g, b).

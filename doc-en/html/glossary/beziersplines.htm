Bezier splines

Bezier splines | Bezier splines
A variation of splines, reference points which are always located on the resultant curve. Extending the functionality of the control points, tangents (tangent points allow you to change the curve without moving the control points.
Professor Pierre Bezier - French engineer and mathematician, worked in the field of machine tools, automatic control and CAD/CAM. In the early 1970s, designing on the computer chassis Cars Renault, applied for the first time for this purpose, the equation, subsequently named his name.

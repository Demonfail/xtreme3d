The computer form of the transfer of the individual component of color. For example, in the color model RGB three channels - red (Red), green (Green) and blue (Blue). Each channel is a numeric value, the range of which depends on the color space. In the case of linear color space in which are usually produced graphical computing on the graphics card, the channel is expressed as the number of the floating-point from 0 to 1. For output to the monitor (in 24-bit mode) channel through the gamma correction is placed in the gamma-ray space and is displayed in a range of integers from 0 to 255.

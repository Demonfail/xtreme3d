Dummycube

Class: TGLDummyCube

The dummy (Dummycube) is a special object, an invisible but having the position and orientation in space. Processing of the dummies is carried out very quickly, and as they, like other objects, may have parents and descendants, this is an ideal objects for the creation of groups and hierarchies. The properties of the manikins and systems constructed on their basis, are applied in areas such as the mechanisms of movement of objects, the hinge design, applying effects and checking the collision.

DummycubeCreate

Real DummycubeCreate =( parent as real );
Creates a new mannequin and returns its id.
Parent - the parent id for the dummy (0 - lack of parents).

DummycubeAmalgamate

Real = DummycubeAmalgamate ( object,mode as real );
Connects all descendants of the dummy in one object in OpenGL. This activates a special mode rendering, in which all the descendants of the manikin will be recorded into one list display. Thus, in some situations, can achieve significant performance gain, but all the changes, addressing the descendants, will be ignored until the dummy will not be caused by ObjectStructureChanged. Some of the objects of the own manage list display, may not be compatible with the DummycubeAmalgamate. In addition, this mode does not allows you to sort and selection of visibility in the usual manner. In short, this option is good for static opaque geometry, or when the camera position does not change over a long period of time.
Object is the id of the dummy
Mode - true or false (1 and 0, respectively).

DummycubeSetCameraMode

Real DummycubeSetCameraMode =( object,cim as real );
Allows you to capture the position (cimPosition) or the orientation of thecimOrientation (dummy) and his descendants in relation to the active camera. That is, in the first case, unable to move away or move closer to the object, the second - remove the object from the view.
Object is the id of the dummy
Cim - cimNone (default value), The cimPosition or cimOrientation (0, 1 or 2, respectively).
